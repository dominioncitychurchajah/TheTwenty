Is this program free?

The program is fully sponsored by the church's building and talent development initiative. There is no cost to participants. The vision is to invest in people first, because the building is only as strong as the people who build it.

Do I need prior tech experience?

No. Beginners are welcome. What matters most is your commitment, curiosity, and willingness to learn. We start from fundamentals and build up. If you can use a smartphone, you can start this journey.

Will I get a job after this program?

No responsible program can guarantee employment. What we can guarantee is access to practical skills, real-world projects, portfolio development, accountability, community support, and proven job-search systems designed to make you more competitive. Your results will largely depend on your commitment, consistency, mindset, and willingness to execute. We will provide the roadmap, tools, training, and support. Your future will be shaped by what you do with them.

How many people will be selected?

Only twenty participants. This ensures personalized support, accountability, and quality outcomes. We believe in depth over breadth.

What happens after I apply?

Application review, then Selection, then Onboarding, then Training. You will receive updates via email and WhatsApp. Selected participants will be invited to an orientation session before the program begins.

What makes this different from other programs?

Because we focus on transformation, not just information. We are not just teaching skills, we are building people who will build the Kingdom. The SHIELD Framework, community accountability, and mission-driven approach set this apart from generic bootcamps.
