An Open Letter To The Next Generation

Dear Future Leader,

We believe the church should prepare people not only for heaven but also for impact here on earth. The Kingdom of God is not just about Sunday services — it is about Monday mornings, Tuesday deadlines, and Friday paychecks.

We are investing in you because we see something in you. Not just what you are now, but what you can become. We see a generation of professionals who will fund missions, build businesses, create jobs, and advance God is work through their careers.

The cathedral we are building is not just brick and mortar. It is a symbol of what happens when a community decides to invest in its people first. When 20 young professionals rise up, earn sustainably, and give back — that is when structures get built, communities get transformed, and legacies get created.

This is your invitation. Not just to learn skills, but to become part of a movement. A movement that believes in stewardship, excellence, and Kingdom impact. A movement that says: "We will build the people before we build the building."

The future is built by people who decide to show up. Will you be one of them?
